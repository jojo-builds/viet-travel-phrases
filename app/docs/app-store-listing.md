## Description (max 4000 chars)

Viet Travel Phrasebook is built for one job: helping you get through real moments in Vietnam without fumbling for signal or typing into a translator.

Open the app and tap into the phrases travelers actually need. Order coffee, buy street food, take a Grab, ask the price, check into a hotel, get directions, and handle simple problems with less guesswork.

Every phrase includes:
- Vietnamese text
- A simple romanized pronunciation guide
- Clear audio playback
- One-tap favorites for quick access later

The app is designed for travel, not classroom study. No lessons to work through. No account to create. No ads to tap past.
