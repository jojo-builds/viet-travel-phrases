## Description (max 4000 chars)

Travel Vietnam with confidence. Vietnamese Travel Phrasebook gives you the exact phrases you need for real travel situations — with native audio pronunciation, romanized guides, and everything available offline.

**No internet needed. No account required. No tracking. Just phrases that work.**

### 🎯 10 Real Travel Scenarios

Each scenario is packed with practical phrases you'll actually use:

1. **☕ Coffee Shop** — Order cà phê sữa đá, egg coffee, coconut coffee, and more. Navigate Vietnam's incredible café culture like a regular.

2. **🍜 Street Food** — Point, order, and pay at street food stalls. Ask about spice levels, get the bill, and compliment the chef.

3. **🚗 Grab & Taxi** — Tell your driver where to go, ask to stop, confirm the fare, and handle common ride situations.

4. **💰 Asking Prices** — Shop at markets and stores with confidence. Ask "how much?", negotiate politely, and understand the answer.

5. **🙏 Polite Basics** — Hello, thank you, sorry, excuse me — the foundation phrases that show respect everywhere you go.

6. **🏪 Convenience Store** — Buy water, SIM cards, snacks, and essentials. Ask for bags, receipts, and find what you need.

7. **🏨 Hotel & Hostel** — Check in, ask about WiFi, request late checkout, report issues, and handle your stay smoothly.

8. **🗺️ Directions** — Ask where things are, understand left/right/straight, and find your way when maps aren't enough.

9. **⚠️ Simple Problems** — Handle emergencies, lost items, health issues, and everyday problems when you need help most.

10. **💬 Small Talk** — Connect with locals beyond transactions. Share where you're from, compliment Vietnam, and make real connections.

### ✨ Features

• **Native Audio** — Hear every phrase pronounced by a native speaker. Tap to play, learn the tones.

• **Romanized Guides** — See the pronunciation written out so you can read and speak even without knowing Vietnamese script.

• **Save Favorites** — Heart the phrases you use most. They're saved to your Favorites tab for quick access.

• **Quick Phrases** — Five essential phrases right on the home screen for instant access to the basics.

• **100% Offline** — Download once, use anywhere. No WiFi at the phở stand? No problem.

• **No Accounts** — Open the app and start learning. No sign-up, no email, no friction.

• **Privacy First** — We collect zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no ads. Your phrasebook is yours alone.

Vietnam is incredible — but the language barrier is real. Most translation apps need internet. Most phrasebooks are too academic. This app gives you exactly what travelers need: real phrases for real situations, with audio you can play for a local if your pronunciation needs backup.
